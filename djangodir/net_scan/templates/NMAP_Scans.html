Command-line flags
  • -v
    • -v stands for Verbose, meaning NMAP will attempt to tell you what it is doing.
  • -sS
    • -sS SYN scan is the default and most popular scan option for good reason. It can be performed quickly, scanning thousands of ports per second on a fast network not hampered by intrusive firewalls.
